Draw Something! Polyline Art

Intructions:

To draw, you simply click within the canvas to create points. The initial point will not be visible on the canvas. The second point will connect to the first point. The third point will connect to the second point and so on and so forth.

Some neat features:

Press 'r' to increase the red in the color of the lines
Press 'R' to decrease the red in the color of the lines
Press 'g' to increase the green in the color of the lines
Press 'G' to decrease the green in the color of the lines
Press 'b' to increase the blue in the color of the lines
Press 'B' to decrease the blue in the color of the lines
Press 'n' to draw a new polyline

Red: 0 | Green: 0 | Blue: 0


Please use a browser that supports the "canvas" tag.
